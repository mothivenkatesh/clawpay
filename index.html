Why deterministic rules, not LLM-per-action

Razorpay burns Claude tokens on every agent action. Works for 50 cart recoveries/day. For 10M accounts in continuous monitoring? Astronomical.

Clawpay: 99% deterministic rules (zero cost). 1% AI edge cases (worth the tokens). AI parser for config generation (one-time).

AI generates the config. Rules execute at zero cost. AI handles edge cases. That's it.
